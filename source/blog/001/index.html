Code / Code / Code

My first blog post ever! My name is Sean and today is the day I start blogging my journey through The Iron Yard Academy. I am hoping that this will serve as a journal of my weekly thoughts, challenges, victories and progress. I hope this also is inspiring others to take action on their dreams. I have spent years wanting to drop everything and learn to code but always had an excuse/reason of why not. I have been itching dreaming of being a full time developer for a long time. Creating something is just one part of my inspiration to become a developer. Not many people wake up to a job they want or even in a field they want. I know that has been my journey in life thus far! I don’t want these blog posts to be incredibly long and I also don’t want to hit you with my life/career story all on entry one. So, we will get back to my story another time. For now what’s happening at TIYA?! Code immersion! It feels like somedays I get it and the next…

It’s a struggle EVERYDAY! I’m not saying this is bad at all. Coming into this I expected the most intense learning pace. I’ll say it again, code immersion! I rarely am doing anything besides something to do with code. Eat/sleep/code sounds hard and it sounds crazy… It’s also awesome, I really try to spend as much time as I can doing something to hone my skills. Week 1 and 2 are really a blur, I have learned tons. It’s been almost entirely Ruby up to this point. Week 2 project was a console blackjack game. It proved to be nearly all Friday, Saturday and Sunday to get it about 90% completed. Best effort is what the aim is for now. I am hoping to come back to it with later learn skills to be much more efficient. Eat/sleep/code isn’t really my story so far. What I’m doing it’s code/code/code. When I’m eating I am watching a tutorial, keynote or reading something! When I’m sleeping I’m dreaming about code. No really! Samantha, my wife, can attest to that. I have an 11 month old daughter, Kennedy, who rarely makes it through the night without needing something. So, last night Samantha asked me to get a diaper, TEAMWORK! Being in that just barely awake 1am frame of mind she tells me I asked her, “what card she needed?” Hilarious! Dreaming of code is real! I did get her the diaper but never found out about what card she wanted.
